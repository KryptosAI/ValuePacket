No user collateral or debt balance may go negative.
Total collateral must equal the sum of all user collateral.
Total debt must equal the sum of all user debt.
The system must be overcollateralized: totalCollateral * liqThreshold >= totalDebt * 10000.
User borrows must maintain individual health: collateral * liqThreshold >= debt * 10000.
User balances and total assets must remain non-negative.
All state-mutating functions use a reentrancy lock.
